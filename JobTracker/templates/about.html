About

The UVic 3D Printing Payment System

This project's goal is to improve the process of requesting a 3D printing job from the University of Victoria's Digital Scholarship Commons. We want to create a seamless user experience from start to finish. Just submit your request and let the software do the rest!

Instructions
In the navigation bar at the top of this webpage, click 'Create Job' to submit a 3D printing job, or click 'View Jobs' to view the status of your print jobs.
